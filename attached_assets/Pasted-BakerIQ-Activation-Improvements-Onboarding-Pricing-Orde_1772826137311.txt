BakerIQ Activation Improvements

(Onboarding + Pricing + Order Page)

We are improving activation and implementation after signup.

These changes should extend existing architecture, not replace it.

Reference: docs/ONBOARDING-WALKTHRU.md

PART 1 — Optimized Onboarding Flow

Current onboarding:

1 Branding
2 Demo Quote
3 Share Order Page
4 Stripe Connect

We will restructure onboarding to better reflect how bakers actually work.

Step 1 — Business Type

Add onboarding step:

What do you sell most?

Options:

Custom Cakes
Treats (cupcakes, strawberries, cake pops, etc)
Both

Save value:

product_mode

Allowed values:

cakes
treats
both
Step 2 — Portfolio Upload

Upload 4–6 images.

Dynamic instructions depending on product_mode.

Cakes
Upload 4–6 cakes you've made recently.
Customers trust bakers when they can see real designs.
Treats
Upload 4–6 treats customers love ordering
(strawberries, cupcakes, cake pops, etc).
Both
Upload a mix of cakes and treats customers love ordering.
Step 3 — Choose Your Pricing Style

Instead of asking for manual pricing numbers, allow bakers to choose a pricing tier.

Explain:

Most professional bakers charge between $5 and $8 per serving.
Choose a starting point. You can adjust everything later.
Cake Pricing Options

Minimal decoration

Custom designs

Luxury

Highly detailed cakes

cake_pricing_tier

Values:

simple
detailed
luxury

Pick the pricing level that best matches your treats.
